TASK 16 – TIME SERIES FORECASTING REPORT

Objective:
To analyze sales trends and forecast future sales using time series methods.

Steps Performed:
• Loaded sales dataset and converted date column
• Aggregated monthly sales
• Visualized trend over time
• Split data into training and testing sets
• Applied Exponential Smoothing forecasting model
• Predicted future sales values
• Evaluated model using MAE
• Exported forecast results to CSV

Results:
The model successfully captured the sales trend and generated reasonable future predictions.

Conclusion:
Time series forecasting helps businesses plan inventory, revenue, and growth strategies effectively.
